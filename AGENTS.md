# Agent Secure Links

## Project Overview

Agent Secure Links is a privacy-first secure link system designed for professionals who collect sensitive information from clients remotely.

Many professionals work with clients over phone calls, video calls, or messaging. During these conversations they often need to collect private information such as Social Security Numbers, banking information, identity details, or other sensitive data.

Clients frequently feel uncomfortable sharing this information verbally due to the risk of scams, identity theft, or privacy concerns. This hesitation creates friction and slows down legitimate transactions.

Agent Secure Links solves this problem by allowing professionals to generate temporary secure links that clients can open on their own device and use to privately submit sensitive information.

Instead of speaking private data aloud, the client enters it directly into a secure encrypted form.

This improves trust, reduces friction, and creates a better experience for both parties.

---

## Core Concept

A professional generates a secure link and sends it to a client during a conversation.

The client opens the link and sees a clean, trustworthy page explaining that they can privately submit their information.

The client enters the information directly into the secure form.

The submission is encrypted and stored securely.

The professional receives the submission inside their dashboard and can review the information safely.

---

## Core Product Principles

This product must prioritize:

Privacy  
Security  
Trust  
Simplicity  

Clients should feel that the system is safer than speaking sensitive information aloud.

The experience should resemble the security expectations of modern fintech products.

---

## Key Capabilities

The system should allow professionals to:

Generate secure submission links

Send links to clients

Allow clients to privately submit sensitive information

View submissions securely

Maintain audit logs for actions such as link creation, opening, and submission

Expire links automatically after a defined period

Optionally delete sensitive data after a retention period

---

## Supported Information Types

The system should support multiple secure form types such as:

Banking information

Social security or national identity information

Basic personal information

General secure document or information submission

Forms should be flexible and expandable.

---

## Banking Verification Feature

When collecting banking information, the system should verify routing numbers using a banking directory or routing number lookup API.

This allows the system to:

Confirm that the routing number corresponds to a valid financial institution

Display the bank name automatically

Provide a better and more trustworthy experience for the user

---

## Trust and User Experience

Client pages should focus on building trust.

Clients should clearly understand:

Why they are entering their information

That the information is encrypted

That the link expires automatically

That they do not need to read private information aloud

The design should feel similar to secure banking or financial platforms.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Over time this platform could expand into a broader trust infrastructure system for professionals who interact with clients remotely.

Potential future directions include:

Client secure portals

Secure document exchange

Identity verification tools

Encrypted client communication

Branded trust pages for professionals

Client vaults for ongoing document storage

Secure intake forms for many industries

The product should remain flexible so it can serve many types of professionals, not just a single industry.

---

## Guiding Philosophy

The product should remain simple.

Security and trust should always come before complexity.

Every feature should reinforce the idea that this is a safer alternative to verbally sharing sensitive information.
